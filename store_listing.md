## Full Description (4000 chars max)

💚 SplitSmart — Your Money, Your Rules.

Split bills, track personal finances, and manage group expenses with zero hassle. Whether you're traveling with friends, sharing an apartment, or just keeping track of your daily spending — SplitSmart handles it all, beautifully and privately.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

👥 GROUP EXPENSE SPLITTING
• Create groups for trips, roommates, dinners, or any shared expense
• Add expenses with custom splits (by percentage, exact amounts, or equal)
• Smart settle-up algorithm calculates the minimum number of payments needed
• See real-time balances — who owes you and who you owe
• Share groups instantly via QR code — scan to join in seconds
• Archive old groups to keep things tidy

💰 PERSONAL FINANCE MANAGER
• Track income & expenses across 90+ world currencies
• Beautiful interactive charts showing your monthly spending by category
• Set weekly or monthly budgets per category with visual progress tracking
• Monitor recurring subscriptions with billing cycle awareness
• Create saving goals and track your progress
• Schedule payment reminders with push notifications

🧠 SMART EXPENSE ENTRY — Zero-Cost AI
• 📷 Receipt Scanner: Point your camera at any receipt — the total is auto-detected using on-device OCR (no internet needed)
• 🎤 Voice Input: Just say "42 euros dinner paid by Ali" — SplitSmart understands
• 💡 Smart Suggestions: Autocomplete from your expense history, no typing needed

🔥 CLOUD SYNC (Optional)
• Sign in with Google or Email to sync across all your devices
• Groups, expenses, and personal data stay in sync via secure cloud storage
• Receipt photos are backed up to the cloud automatically
• Get instant push notifications when group members add expenses
• Works perfectly offline — data syncs when you're back online

🔒 PRIVACY & SECURITY
• Biometric lock (Fingerprint / Face ID) protects your financial data
• Compressed encrypted backups — export and import your entire database
• Privacy-safe analytics — we track feature usage only, never your financial data
• No ads, no tracking, no data selling — ever

🌍 GLOBAL READY
• Available in 8 languages: English, Urdu, Arabic, French, Spanish, German, Turkish, Hindi
• Full RTL support for Arabic and Urdu
• 90+ currencies with correct symbols and formatting

📊 EXPORT & REPORTS
• Generate professional PDF reports for groups and personal transactions
• Share summaries via WhatsApp, Telegram, Email, or any messaging app
